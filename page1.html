Missing Child Identification Portal

Find-Fast is an advanced, automated system designed to assist in the rapid identification and recovery of missing children using AI. This project integrates Machine Learning and facial recognition to analyze and cross-reference images from public surveillance, social media, and law enforcement databases in real-time, identifying potential matches with missing child records. By enhancing the speed and precision of search efforts, it aims to shorten response times and improve outcomes in child recovery cases.

The platform provides secure, encrypted data handling and complies with stringent privacy standards to protect sensitive information. Designed for use by law enforcement, child protection agencies, and non-profit organizations, Find-Fast also enables the distribution of targeted alerts, keeping communities informed and mobilized in the search for missing children. This system represents a significant advancement in utilizing technology for child protection and public safety.
